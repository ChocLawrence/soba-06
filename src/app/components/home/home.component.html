About Sasse College

The South West Region is home to Sasse College, with a lovely landscape and pleasing scenery. Saint Joseph's College, Sasse (Sasse College or Republic of Sasse) is the first secondary school in the Cameroons. It is a secondary school in the South West Region of Cameroon, with its campus located in Sasse, Buea. It was created in 1939 by the Mill Hill Missionaries.
